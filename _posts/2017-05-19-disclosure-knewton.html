"Pearson, an Investor in Knewton, Is 'Phasing Out' Partnership on Adaptive Products," Edsurge wrote on May 12, 2017. This article fails to disclose that Edsurge and Knewtonshare investors: GSV. Pearson is also one of the largest limited partners in Learn Capital, another Edsurge investor.

This sort of disclosure (or lack thereof) is particularly important as Edsurge now sells schools a service recommending which ed-tech products they should buy.

This article helps provide funding data for part 9 of my annual review of the year in ed-tech

The following startups that market themselves as selling “character education” curriculum and “social emotional learning” raised funding in 2016.

  • Mightifier ($250,000) – investors include xEdu and Courage Ventures Seed 1
  • ClassDojo ($20,000,000) – investors include General Catalyst, GSV, Reach Capital, and SignalFire

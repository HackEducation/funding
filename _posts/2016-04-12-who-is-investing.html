I’m starting a new report on ed-tech startup funding – it’s related to this one – that tracks all investors and all their education-related investments since 2009 (the year I started Hack Education).

I haven’t quite decided yet how I’ll present the data – I think sorted by investor, but the point will be that I can provide an ongoing update to stories like “Who’s Investing in Ed-Tech?”

This data will also be made available – freely and openly – in a JSON file so that others can access and utilize it.

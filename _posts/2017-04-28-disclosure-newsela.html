"Data Sharing Hasn't Been a Concern for Most Newsela Customers -- But Super Users Want More," Edsurge wrote on April 24, 2017. This article fails to disclose that Edsurge and Newsela share investors: Reach Capital and NewSchools Venture Fund.

This sort of disclosure (or lack thereof) is particularly important as Edsurge now sells schools a service in which it recommends ed-tech products that it says schools should buy.
